Types of Solar Panels

There are three main types:

  • Monocrystalline: High efficiency (15-22%), made from single silicon crystals; ideal for limited space.
  • Polycrystalline: Affordable, 13-15% efficiency; made from multiple silicon fragments.
  • Thin-Film: Flexible and lightweight, lower efficiency (7-13%); good for portable applications.

Choose based on budget, space, and efficiency needs.
